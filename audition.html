Welcome! Please select an available time slot. If you are planning to participate in Panhel Recruitment, we recommend auditioning on Friday to avoid conflicts.

Preparation

Each audition is 15 minutes long and includes a range check followed by a solo of your choice. The solo should be about 90 seconds in length, or about a verse and a chorus. This is where you really get to strut your stuff, so pick a song that really shows off your voice! This solo is the only thing you need to prepare in advance. If you have any questions, just email us at choral-info@mit.edu!
