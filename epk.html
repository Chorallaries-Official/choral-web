About us

We are the chorallaries

We are MIT’s first co-ed a cappella group! We perform in the Boston/Cambridge area, tour the country, and go on retreat to Vermont! We also perform at various competitions like the International Championship of Collegiate A Cappella (ICCA). We focus on sound, musicality and performance and we have a great time singing together!
